# Unit 5 – Activity 1: App Idea Brainstorming

## Step 1 – Brainstorming Ideas

### Idea 1 – Daily Boost (Quotes + Mood)
Shows a daily inspirational quote from an API. Users can save favorites and log their mood (happy/okay/sad).

### Idea 2 – Tennis Session Tracker
Tracks tennis practices and matches. Users can log opponent, score, date, and notes. Basic stats like hours played or wins.

### Idea 3 – Meal Randomizer
User chooses a cuisine and gets a random recipe from a public recipes API. Users can save favorite meals.

### Idea 4 – Simple Study Planner
Students add classes and tasks. Shows a list of homework/exams with due dates and allows checking items as completed.

### Idea 5 – Habit Checklist
Users pick daily habits and mark them as done. Shows streaks and simple progress.

### Idea 6 – Water Reminder & Tracker
Users log the cups of water they drink each day. The app tracks daily progress toward a hydration goal and shows simple weekly progress. Optional reminders help users stay hydrated throughout the day.

---

## Step 2 – Top 3 Ideas

1. **Daily Boost**  
2. **Tennis Session Tracker**  
3. **Meal Randomizer**

---

## Step 2 – Evaluation

### 1) Daily Boost (Quotes + Mood)
**Mobile:** Well suited for mobile; fast daily use, supports notifications.  
**Story:** Very easy to understand and motivates users daily.  
**Market:** Anyone who enjoys quotes or simple wellness apps.  
**Habit:** Encourages daily use through quotes and mood logging.  
**Scope:** Very manageable—just a few screens + one simple API.

---

### 2) Tennis Session Tracker
**Mobile:** Great for users on the tennis court.  
**Story:** Clear value for players and coaches tracking performance.  
**Market:** Smaller niche but strong usefulness for tennis players.  
**Habit:** Used regularly after practices or matches.  
**Scope:** Simple CRUD features + light statistics.

---

### 3) Meal Randomizer
**Mobile:** Very useful when choosing what to eat.  
**Story:** Fun and fast meal discovery.  
**Market:** Wide audience—anyone who cooks.  
**Habit:** Used a few times per week when planning meals.  
**Scope:** Requires API integration + favorites list.

---

## Step 2.3 – Final Decision

### **Final App Idea:** Daily Boost – Quotes + Mood Tracker

**Why this idea was chosen:**
- Very small and clear scope  
- Uses a free quote API  
- Simple and fast to build in SwiftUI  
- Encourages daily engagement and habit building  
- Offers real value to students and general users
